Frequently Asked Questions

Few questions that are commonly asked.

  • Where can I download WPortfolio?

    WPortfolio is available exclusively only on Envato Market. Please open Installation page for further details.

  • How to change the color scheme?

    WPortfolio provide its SCSS files to let us modify and changes the stylesheet of the theme. Please refer to Customization page for further details.

  • How to add a custom section in front page?

    WPortfolio provide lot of hooks to modify the theme to meet our needs. In order to add custom section, we just need to add filter to wportfolio_data_sections hook. This also has been documented in Customization page and even given as an example in the child theme.
